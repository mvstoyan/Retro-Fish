About us

RETRO FISH is the hard work of the two passionate founders with humble beginnings: a self-taught chef and a restaurateur. Together, their passion for creating a unique culinary experience is what they can’t wait to share with your family and friends, near or far.

Established in 1988 in Bigcity, is not just a seafood restaurant. It’s a totally private retro venue known for its extraordinary cuisine, amazing live music, warm casual elegance and friendly, professional service.
